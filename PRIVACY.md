# Flux Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-05-12

Flux is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use and protect your information.

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal information about you. This includes, but is not limited to:

- No account creation or login required
- No user profiles
- No tracking across websites
- No personal identifiers
- No browsing history
- No usage statistics

## 2. Information We Collect

The only data collected by Flux is for the sole purpose of providing its functionality:

- **Currency exchange rates**: Fetched from an external API for conversion purposes.
- **User preferences**: Your selected currencies, theme choice, and disabled sites are stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`. This data never leaves your device.

This data is used strictly to enable currency conversion and does not identify or track users.

## 3. How We Use Information

Your information is used only for the following purposes:

- Converting currency values displayed on websites
- Providing real-time exchange rate updates
- Maintaining user-selected currency preferences (locally stored in browser)

## 4. Data Storage

- Currency exchange rates are cached temporarily in the browser's local storage to improve performance and reduce API calls.
- All user preferences are stored locally and are never transmitted externally.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

- We do not share, sell, or rent any user information.
- Your personal data is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.
- The only external communication is when fetching currency exchange rates from the specified API.

## 6. Third-Party Services

Flux uses the following third-party service:

- **Currency API**: [fawazahmed0/currency-api](https://github.com/fawazahmed0/currency-api)

Please refer to the API provider's privacy policy for information on their data handling practices.

## 7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

- Browser-level data encryption
- Secure API communication (HTTPS only)
- Regular security updates

We are committed to maintaining the security and integrity of our extension.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
